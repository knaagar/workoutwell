BMI Calculator

BMI (Body Mass Index) is used to assess a person's weight relative to their height and is commonly used to screen for general health and estimate whether an individual is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

Waist-Hip Ratio Calculator

WHR (Waist-Hip Ratio) is used to assess the distribution of fat in the body and is often used as an indicator of the risk of developing various health conditions, particularly related to abdominal or central obesity. A high WHR may indicate a higher risk of certain health issues such as cardiovascular disease and type 2 diabetes.

BMR Calculator



BMR, or Basal Metabolic Rate, is the amount of energy (in calories) that your body needs to maintain basic functions while at rest. It represents the energy your body requires to perform vital functions such as breathing, circulating blood, regulating body temperature, and supporting various biochemical reactions necessary for life. BMR is typically measured in calories per day and is influenced by factors like age, gender, weight, height, and body composition. It's an essential component in understanding your daily caloric needs and can be used as a basis for determining calorie requirements for weight management and overall health.
